An Intelligent Assistant for School Administrators

Designed to save time, minimize errors, optimize costs, and enhance staff morale by turning unstructured documents into actionable scheduling data.

The Problem: The Daily Scramble

Every morning, Assistant Principals spend 60-90 minutes juggling spreadsheets, making frantic phone calls, and manually assigning substitute teachers. This reactive process is inefficient, costly, and pulls leadership away from instructional priorities.

The Solution: Automated Intelligence

Harry Llama ingests your school's unique schedules and absence reports. Our AI engine then automates the entire assignment process based on your rules, finding the best coverage in minutes, not hours. Reclaim up to 87% of your scheduling time and focus on leadership.

Core Features

Everything needed to automate & streamline substitute management.

AI-Powered Processing

Interprets the rules and context of your master schedule, analyzes daily absence reports, and assign substitutes.

Comprehensive Onboarding

A guided 7-step wizard configures the application to your school's specific needs, including schedule rotation, bell schedules, district closing calendar, and co-teacher identification.

4-Tier Intelligent Scheduling
